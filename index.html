About SITHELO NPO

For over 16 years, we have walked alongside women, youth and children in Durban — igniting hope, building resilience and transforming lives through purpose-driven community action.

Our Story

SITHELO — Sivusa Ithemba Lembokodo — was established in 2008 in Montclair, Durban, born from a simple but powerful conviction: that every woman, every child and every young person deserves the opportunity to flourish.

What began as a small community gathering has grown into a registered NPO with structured programmes reaching hundreds of beneficiaries across the Durban South region and beyond.

Our name means "reviving the hope of a strong woman" — a declaration that underpins every programme, initiative and relationship we build.

What Drives Us

  • Women deserve economic independence and social equality
  • Youth need direction, mentorship and real opportunity
  • Children require safety, nutrition and access to education
  • Communities grow stronger when they support one another
  • Skills are the most durable form of empowerment
  • Every voice in the community matters and deserves to be heard

Our Vision

A society in which every woman stands in her power, every young person has a clear and hopeful path forward, and every child grows up in safety, dignity and love.

Our Mission

To ignite and sustain hope in communities through women empowerment, youth development, children's welfare and skills training — creating a ripple of positive change that extends across generations.

"Sivusa Ithemba Lembokodo — reviving the hope of a strong woman. This is not just our name. It is our promise."

— SITHELO NPO, Est. 2008

Privacy Policy

How SITHELO NPO collects, uses, and protects your data.

Introduction

At SITHELO NPO, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard the personal information you provide to us, particularly through our Contact and Volunteer forms.

Information We Collect

When you interact with our forms, we may collect the following personal information:

  • Name and Surname: To identify and address you personally.
  • Email Address: To respond to your inquiries and communicate updates.
  • Phone Number: To contact you regarding volunteer opportunities or urgent matters.

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used strictly for fulfilling your requests and keeping you engaged with our mission. We use it to:

  • Process volunteer applications and coordinate activities.
  • Respond to general inquiries, partnership proposals, and support messages.
  • Send important updates related to our programmes and community initiatives.

Data Security

We implement robust security measures to ensure that your personal information is kept safe. Access to your data is restricted to authorized SITHELO NPO personnel only, and we adhere to strict confidentiality protocols.

Third-Party Services

We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Your information is only shared with trusted third-party services that directly support our operations (such as our email delivery system), and these providers are bound by strict privacy and data protection agreements.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy or your data, please contact us at info@sithelonpo.co.za.
